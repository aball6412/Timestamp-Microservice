Timestamp Microservice


How to Use:

Pass a natural language date or a untix timestap as a parameter to the URL.

In return, you will receive JSON that converts the natural language date to a unix timestamp, or converts a unix timestamp to a natural language date.


Example:

https://quiet-lake-40342.herokuapp.com/December15,2015
https://quiet-lake-40342.herokuapp.com/1450137600

https://quiet-lake-40342.herokuapp.com/December9,1988
https://quiet-lake-40342.herokuapp.com/597628800

Example Output:

{"Unixdate":1450137600,"Natural Date":"December 15, 2015"}
